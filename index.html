About

How this site was born.

  • 2010

    The Year I Became A Car Enthusiast

    Back in 2010 my father took me the my very first car event and I was instanlty obsessed with cars. For the rest of youth I was always looking around and trying my best to name the cool cars that I saw.

  • 2014

    Automotive Videography Journey Begins

    Once I got my first smart phone, I began filming just about every cool car that I saw. There was something really exciting about documenting it.

  • 2017

    Transition to YouTube

    With all the cool car videos I had, I decided to start posting them online. Doing so allowed me to share my passion with others in addition to growing my social media presence.

  • 2022

    Matt Mehran's Top Car Picks Is Born

    The fun I had sharing my passion with others over YouTube motivated me to create this website where I can share some of the best cars to own. My goal is to inspire future car enthusiasts in addition to educating my fellow car loving people!

1. Koenigsegg Jesko

Price: $3.0 Million

The Koenigsegg Jesko is a one of a kind car that is capable of reaching 60 MPH in just 2.5 seconds from a standstill. This kind of performance is made possible by its twin-turbocharged 5.0L V-8 that produces 1280 horsepower. This vehicle also looks incredible from any angle due to its unique body lines and aggressive aero. Lastly, the exhaust note on this car is unreal and it will definitely turn every head on the block. Given this, the Koenigsegg Jesko earns the number 1 spot on this list.

2. Pagani Huayra Dinastia

Price: $4.6 Million

The Pagani Huayra Dinastia is as close to a piece of art as a vehicle can get. Don’t let its beauty distract you, however, from the fact that it is a super aggressive performance vehicle at heart. It is capable of reaching 60 MPH from a standstill in only 3.1 seconds making it extremely quick. If those performance stats weren’t enough to justify the number 2 spot on this list, maybe its full carbon fiber body is. Not only does it contribute to weight savings, but it largely makes this car one of the craziest looking things to ever hit the road. To top it off, this car’s exhaust screams louder than just about any other car on the road due to the fact that it has a massive V-12 engine that makes 730 horsepower.

3. Lamborghini Aventador SVJ

Price: $517,000

The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ is no slouch when it comes to performance. Its massive 6.5L V-12 engine rockets the vehicle to 60 in only 2.5 seconds from a standstill. As far as appearances go, it looks quite insane. While all the features I’ve mentioned so far are impressive, the exhaust sound really sets this car apart. This vehicle has a super distinct sound that is enjoyed by car enthusiasts around the globe. Given that this vehicle is solid all around, it definitely deserves its spot at number 3 on the list.

4. Lamborghini Huracan Performante

Price: $206,000

The Lamborghini Huracan is another great performer with a 0-60 time of just 2.8 seconds. This vehicle can also be modified pretty easily to be even faster which is another significant plus. Just like all the other cars on this list, the Huracan is a great looking vehicle with sporty body lines. Finally, this car has a very powerful V-10 motor that sounds quite amazing. All around the Huracan is a solid car, justifying its number 4 spot on the list.

5. Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG Black Series

Price: $187,000

The Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG Black Series is a super powerful car for being naturally aspirated. Its 6.3L V-8 produces 622 horsepower which gets the car to 60 in 3.2 seconds from a standstill. This car really stands out though due to its unique body style. It is the only front engine car on this list and it features a massive hood that makes this thing look absolutely menacing. As one would expect, its V-8 motor creates a very bold sound that is sure to put a smile on the face of any driver. Considering all of the impressive attributes, this car finds itself in spot number 5.

6. Porsche 911 GT3RS

Price: $185,000

The Porsche 911 GT3RS is super quick and it handles especially well. This car only has six cylinders, but it still manages to accomplish a 0-60 time of just 3.2 seconds. This car has a super aggressive look due to its huge wing. If you are a Porsche enthusiast, then you can definitely appreciate all the unique styling characteristics. It also has a super distinct exhaust note that all car enthusiasts recognize and love. Given this, the GT3RS definitely deserves spot number 6 on this list.
